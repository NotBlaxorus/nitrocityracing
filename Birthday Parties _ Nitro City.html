FAQ

Common Questions from Moms

What ages can race?

Packages on this page are tuned for ages 6–13. Younger racers get lower speeds and extra guidance.

What should kids wear?

Closed‑toe shoes are required. We recommend comfy, fitted clothing; helmets are provided.

Can we bring our own cake and decorations?

Yes! You’re welcome to bring cake and decor. Please use painter’s tape for walls; confetti is typically not allowed.

Do you have food and drink options?

Yes—kid‑approved menus and drink bundles are available, or you can bring your own. Check the Packages page for details.

Is axe throwing available for kids?

Yes—axe throwing is available for ages 12 and up only. A safety briefing is required before play.

Are kart speeds limited by age?

No. Kart speeds are not limited by age. We tailor race formats and provide instruction to match driver experience and keep everyone safe.
